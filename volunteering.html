Volunteering With SSS
The Scholarly Service Society, as a non-profit organization, relies on volunteers to ensure its smooth running and the meeting of its goals.
Joining a Chapter
One way to start volunteering is by joining a Chapter, where you can find volunteer opportunities that align with your interests, helping the community in the process. You can join the google classroom for the chapter you are interested in volunteering for, and make sure to contact scholarlyservicesociety@gmail.com in case you have any queries.
Our Current Chapters Are:
  1. A chapter of the Scholarly Service Society is tasked with aligning its members with volunteer opportunities that correspond to their fields of interest. For example, chapters can focus on volunteering sectors such as business, STEM, medicine, law, computer science, and more. Chapter leaders are responsible for creating a Google Classroom to regularly post volunteer opportunities related to various fields.
Starting a Chapter
Can't find a chapter near you or want to do more? Why not start your own chapter? If you would like to start a chapter in your area, please fill out the form below. We will review your application and let you know of the next steps involved. Make sure to also send us an email at scholarlyservicesociety@gmail.com to confirm your interest.
