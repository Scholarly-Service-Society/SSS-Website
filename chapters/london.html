SSS London
The Scholarly Service Society, London Chapter was the first international chapter of the SSS. We organise talks and events with the goal of creating opportunities for young people, such as webinars and panels with University Students and Professionals. In 2025, we organised the 'Futures in STEM' Competition sponsored by Leaf Courses, creating a platform for students to explore STEM and address global challenges, reaching 100+ students and securing nearly 40 innovative project submissions.
