What is a Chapter?
Purpose of Chapters
The primary goal of establishing chapters of the Scholarly Service Society is to broaden the impact of our initiative, reaching and assisting as many individuals as possible. We aim to empower teenagers worldwide to foster cleaner, safer, and more educated communities through dedicated volunteerism.
What Does a Chapter Do?
  1. A chapter of the Scholarly Service Society is tasked with aligning its members with volunteer opportunities that correspond to their fields of interest. For example, chapters can focus on volunteering sectors such as business, STEM, medicine, law, computer science, and more. Chapter leaders are responsible for creating a Google Classroom to regularly post volunteer opportunities related to various fields.
  2. Chapters are also encouraged to organize their own community service events, engaging their members in activities that benefit the community. These events should align with our mission of creating cleaner, safer, and more educated communities through volunteerism.
