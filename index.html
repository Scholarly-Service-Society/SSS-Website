About Us
SSS was started by Pahul Singh Virk and Yusuf Dagli in New Jersey, and is quickly expanding internationally in order to create a more global impact. We are a youth-led nonprofit, and aim to continually strive to make a positive difference for everyone through the power of volunteering and teamwork.
Scholarly Service Society is a youth-led nonprofit organization dedicated to creating cleaner, safer, and more educated communities through volunteerism. Our esteemed mission encompasses matching teens with volunteer opportunities aligned with their interests and aspirations. By hosting numerous community service initiatives, we not only support those in need but also equip young individuals with valuable experience and skills related to their chosen fields. If you want to join us in our mission, be sure to explore starting a new chapter or creating a partnership with us.
